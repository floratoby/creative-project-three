John Smith

John Smith runs a successful Dunkin Donuts coffeehouse in Fairfax VA. He is divorced with 3 children of which he currently pays $1500 in alimony and child support. He has been working at this coffeehouse since his senior year in high school. He worked his way from porter all the way up to store manager and now owner. He is hardworking and was always available to pick up shifts when his co-workers called out. His whole life revolved around Dunkin Donuts. When he found out the owner was selling it, even though it was located in a high crime area, he offered to purchase it and used all the money he was saving for college as a down payment. He has now been the owner of Dunkin Donuts for 25 years. The last owner was a police officer who worked for the FCPD and the majority of his customers were also police officers. John knows that 80% of his revenue comes from police officers.
